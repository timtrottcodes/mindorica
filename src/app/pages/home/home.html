What is Mindorica?

A Personal Learning Companion

Mindorica is a powerful flashcard and quiz tool designed for focused, intentional learning. Whether you're studying a new language, revising for exams, or building long-term knowledge, Mindorica adapts to your goals. It was born from a desire to learn Bulgarian using modern tools, and now offers flexible features for learners of all kinds.

Smart Flashcards with Multiple Choice

Create flashcards with text, images, and audio. For deeper engagement, you can add multiple-choice options to each card via a simple drag-and-drop interface. Mindorica remembers your choices and provides immediate feedback to support active recall and spaced repetition.

Quick Import, Export, and Demo Data

Easily import and export your flashcards as JSON or ZIP (with media). You can also load sample decks to explore features: try a basic German vocabulary set or a fun general knowledge quiz right from the app.

Organize by Topic

Group your cards by topic for better structure and clarity. Topics can be nested, making it easy to manage large decks or different areas of study. Stay in control of your learning journey with flexible organization.

Built with Angular 20 and Modern Web Tech

Mindorica is built using Angular 20 with standalone components, TypeScript, and modern best practices. It runs entirely in your browser using localStorage, with no signup or server required.

Distraction-Free Learning

Mindorica offers a calm, focused interface designed to help you stay present. Review cards at your pace, flip them with keyboard shortcuts, and track your progress with smart rating tools.
